---
title: "Accept Privacy Policy"
description: "Automatically accept the privacy policy for the Dashboard without having to give consent in the console."
---

```js
new DBD.Dashboard({
  acceptPrivacyPolicy: Boolean,
});
```

## Example Usage

```javascript
new DBD.Dashboard({
  acceptPrivacyPolicy: true,
});
```

## Props

<ParamField path="acceptPrivacyPolicy" type="boolean" required>
  Whether or not to automatically accept the privacy policy.
</ParamField>
